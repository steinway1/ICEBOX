Do I Need To Schedule an Appointment?

Appointments are not required, but they are preferred. If you would like to book an appointment, please contact Jenee via cell 404-991-9721, or email jenee.ewen@icebox.com Typical business hours are Monday-Friday 11:00am-7:00pm.

What Can I Sell To Icebox?

We buy Gold, Bullion, Rolex, Audemars Piguet, Patek Philippe, Silver Flatware, Diamonds, and GIA Diamonds. Additionally, we also service and loans Rolex, Diamonds, and jewelry.

How Long Does It Take?

The entire process typically takes less than twenty minutes. We use state of the art equipment and software to streamline the gold buying process to make your visit efficient and enjoyable.

Will I Receive Cash On The Spot?

Yes! We provide a cash offer for the item(s) during your visit. If you prefer, you may also opt for payment via check.

How Do You Determine The Value Of My Piece(s)?

Several factors determine the cash value for gold, diamonds, and watches. This includes market demand, melt values, wholesale values, etc. Rest assured we offer fair value and the best prices in town!

What Should I Bring?

In order to sell jewelry or watches, you must provide a valid form of government ID. It is also strongly recommended you bring any paperwork you have for the item(s) such as appraisals, GIA certificates, the original box, and serial card.

Do You Buy Broken Items?

Yes, we provide offers for gold and diamond jewelry in all conditions.

Who Do I Contact For Further Details?

If you have further questions/concerns, please call or text Jenee at 404-991-9721.
