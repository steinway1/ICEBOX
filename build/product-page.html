What materials are your products made of?

Our pieces are crafted from high-quality metals, including 14k gold (available in rose, white, or yellow), as well as other premium metals like platinum and stainless steel.

Are the products safe for sensitive skin?

Absolutely! Our products are completely hypoallergenic and safe for all skin types.

How can I keep my jewelry in good condition?

Regular care is essential. We recommend using the special cleaning products available on our site to maintain the shine and durability of your pieces.

Is there a warranty on your products?

Yes, we offer a one-year limited warranty that covers significant manufacturing defects and normal wear and tear. Please note, customer negligence and damage to diamonds over 0.10ct are not covered. Icebox reserves the right to repair or replace items at its discretion.

How do I repair my jewelry?

Our repairs are warranted for six months from the repair date. This warranty covers defects in materials or workmanship on the specific repairs we handle. It excludes normal wear and tear, invisible set items, and diamonds over 0.10ct.

Do you offer multiple payment options?

Definitely! We accept a variety of payment methods including credit and debit cards, PayPal, BitPay, Zelle, and wire transfers. Simply email orders@icebox.com, and our Online Sales Team will assist you with the payment process.

Wrist Diameter

Find the perfect size for you by measuring your wrist.

1. You can use a measuring tape, strip of paper or length of string/yarn as a measurement tool.

2. Wrap one of these around your wrist where you plan to wear the bracelet and mark where it joins.

3. Lay your measuring tool on a flat surface and measure the length up to the mark.

4. Use our measurement chart to determine the closest Icebox bracelet size.

Color

Diamond color refers to how clear a diamond is. Diamond color is a crucial aspect of your diamond's appearance.

K - Near colorless

The color may be visible to the unaided eye. Diamonds with a K-color grade can be a smart choice, offering excellent value for money.

Clarity

Diamond Clarity refers to the absence of inclusions and blemishes.

I1, I2, I3 - Included

Diamonds in this clarity range have inclusions that are easily visible under 10x magnification and may also be visible to the naked eye. These diamonds may have reduced brilliance and sparkle due to their inclusions.

Determine Finger Diameter

Find the perfect ring size for you by measuring the diameter of your finger.

1. You can use a measuring tape, strip of paper or length of string/yarn as a measurement tool.

2. Wrap one of these around your finger where you plan to wear the ring and mark where it joins.

3. Lay your measuring tool on a flat surface and measure the length up to the mark using milimmeters (mm).

4. Use our measurement chart to determine the closest Icebox ring size.

Find The Length

Find the perfect size for you by measuring your neck.

1. String or yarn is the best measurement tool, but you can use a measuring tape or strip of paper.

2. Place it around your neck where you plan to wear the necklace and mark where it joins.

Your Necklace Length

Find the perfect size for you by measuring necklace.

1. Take your necklace you choose for measuring
